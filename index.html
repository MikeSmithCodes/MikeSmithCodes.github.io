Welcome!

Hey there! I'm Mike, and you've stumbled upon my digital portfolio. Welcome!!!

Kick your shoes off and feel free to look around!

The "What"

I plan to grow this website over time. Let's say roughly 1 page per gray hair. That said, the content may change, narrow in on a specific topic, or even get a bit messy. For now the idea is to capture some of the follow: personal projects, a little about myself, how you can reach me, and some miscellaneous content.

I will not be sharing my professional work samples, for obvious reasons, and similarly, I will not be sharing much of the code I wrote in graduate school. Those projects may be reused over the years and I want to prevent academic dishonesty. The struggle is where we grow! You can do it!

The "Why"

You might be wondering why this website exists and if so, wow, you're so inquisitive! Well let me tell you... Growing up my parents prioritized education over everything else. I've learned a fair amount and now I want to give back. Perhaps my projects can be quickly adapted to benefit you or perhaps they can inspire new curiousities. Maybe my reading list will lead you to some exciting new topics. Better yet, maybe you will follow in my path and create your own portfolio to share with, and inspire, others!

Go explore!

If you do have feedback, suggestions, or just want to drop a line about how this might have helped you, feel free to reach out.

Enjoy!
